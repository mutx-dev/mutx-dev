# Quality Gates — MUTX X Posting Rules

## Volume Rules

| Type | Limit per Week |
|---|---|
| Original posts | Max 2 |
| Quote-RTs | Max 7 |
| Replies | Unlimited (if threshold met) |

**Why 2 originals max?** Quality and distinctiveness degrade with frequency. Each original must earn its place with a genuine milestone or new positioning angle.

## Quality Thresholds

### Replies
- **Minimum follower threshold:** 50 (account must have 50+ followers) — OR —
- **Minimum engagement threshold:** 20 (post must have 20+ likes/reposts combined)
- **Preference:** Verified accounts with substantive in-lane content
- **Best targets:** Zero-reply threads from verified builders in MUTX's lane — maximal white space

### Quote-RTs
- **Minimum follower threshold:** 200 (account must have 200+ followers) — AND —
- **Minimum view threshold:** 30 (post must have 30+ views at time of action)
- **Must add:** The production/governance layer the original post doesn't cover
- **Never quote:** Competitors' product launches, startup self-promotion

### Original Posts
- **Must be grounded** in a real commit, release, or verifiable product truth
- **Must add distinct positioning** not covered in recent posts (check posts_ready.md)
- **Must pass enterprise voice check** (no dev-coded phrasing)
- **Must be genuinely newsworthy** — not manufactured urgency
- **50-minute minimum gap** between any two original posts (own posts)

## What Counts as "Genuinely Newsworthy"

✅ **Yes:**
- Major release (v1.1, v1.0, major feature)
- First-of-category positioning (clear category stake)
- Commits with significant operational implications (auth fixes, self-healing, ownership enforcement)
- Verified production insight that adds to current discourse

❌ **No:**
- Routine commits that don't change the operator story
- Platform announcements that aren't grounded in shipped product
- Takes on trends that don't connect to MUTX's lane

## Thread Posting Method

**CRITICAL: The plus button / "add tweet" button is broken. Do not use it.**

### Correct Method
1. Post tweet 1 of the thread (normal compose → Post)
2. Navigate to tweet 1 on the profile
3. Click Reply on tweet 1
4. Write tweet 2 in the reply box
5. Post tweet 2
6. Repeat: navigate to tweet 2, reply, write tweet 3, etc.

### Why This Works
- X threads are just reply chains — replying to your own tweet creates a thread
- The thread shows up correctly on profile and in notifications
- The "add tweet" button causes inconsistent rendering and is unreliable

## 50-Minute Rule

Between any two original posts (own posts), wait **50+ minutes minimum**.
- This prevents dilution and ensures each post gets space to land
- Quote-RTs and replies do NOT reset this clock
- Only original posts count

## Enterprise Voice Checklist

Before posting, verify:
- [ ] No dev-coded phrases: "real install", "actual bootstrap", "not just vibes", "3am", "hangs in a weird half-state"
- [ ] Lead with what the operator gains or prevents
- [ ] No hedging words: "very", "quite", "basically"
- [ ] Buyer/procurement language where appropriate
- [ ] If CLI/infra mentioned: context explains why it matters to an operator, not just a builder
